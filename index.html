Step 1:

Ensure that your seeds are suitable to plant during your current season. View options here

  • Peas can be planted in winter
  • Lettuce can be planted in spring
  • Carrots can be planted in summer.
  • Broccoli can be planted in autumn

Step 2:

Dig small holes with your fingertips.

Place a seed in each small hole.

Step 3:

Cover the holes lightly with soil.

  • Don't put more than 1cm of soil above each seed.

Step 4:

Sprinkle the newly planted seeds with a small amount of water. Don't use too much or it will wash the seeds away.

Make sure the seeds will be in a position with enough sunlight.

Step 5:

Keep an eye on your seeds. They will most likely start sprouting between 1-2 weeks after planting them.

Water them regularly and replant after thjeyve grown taller than 10cm.
